Best Education

Ol'lessos Technical Institute is a public TVET institution located in Nandi County, mandated and accredited to train various Technical and Business courses. The institute is ISO 9001:2015 certified and it strives in insuring excellence in execution of its programmes which is achieved through preparing trainees with relevant knowledge and skills as the fundamental building blocks for better opportunities. Our programmes are now fully competence based(CBET) in line with the Government's directive. The enrollment has progressively grown over the years to the current enrollment of 7535, with over 10,290 trainees having graduated.
